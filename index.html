Health Status of Northeast Texas

This interactive tool is a digital complement to the 2021 Northeast Texas Health Status Report. It is intended to provide a reference for stakeholders in Northeast Texas, the 35-county area also referred to as the DSHS Health Service Region 4/5. It provides and contextualizes data for all-cause mortality as well as the top five leading causes of death in the region.
